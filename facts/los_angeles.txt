Los Angeles (US: /lɔːs ˈændʒələs/ (About this soundlisten) lawss AN-jəl-əs; Tongva: Tovaangar; Spanish: 'Los Ángeles'; 'The Angels'), officially the City of Los Angeles and often abbreviated as L.A., is the largest city in California. It has an estimated population of nearly 4 million, and is the second-largest city in the United States, after New York City, and the third-largest city in North America, after Mexico City and New York City.
